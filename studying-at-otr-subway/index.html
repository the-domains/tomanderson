Studying at OTR Subway

Sadly there are not many places to work after 9 pm in Adelaide. Most coffee shops close at 6 or 9 pm, but I have found an exception. OTR convenient stores are 24 hours, and although some locations are drive-through, others have seating areas and restaurants like Subway. And the best part is that they are open 24 hours a day. There's no power points available, but as long as my laptop is charged up, I can pull late hour writing sessions with free soda refills!
